Test III. Farm animals. The swine (III)

ТESTS OF LIVESTOCK PRODUCTION AND PROCESSING TECHNOLOGIES OF ANIMAL PRODUCTS

USE ALL YOUR KNOWLEDGES FOR DOING THIS TEST!

Choose the correct word in each of the following:
1. What tense is it?
“Hogs have been domesticated as sources of food, leather and similar products since ancient times”?
a) Present Indefinite;
b) Present Continuous;
c) Present Perfect;
d) Past Indefinite;
2. What word is odd?
“Pigs can to be trained to perform numerous simple tasks and tricks”
a) can;
b) to;
c) be;
d) trained;
3. “Pigs ____ since ancient times in the Old World”.
a) are domesticated;
b) were domesticated;
c) have been domesticated;
d) had been domesticated;
4. What word is wrong?
“The domestic pig is farmed for its meat calling pork”.
a) farmed;
b) for;
c) its;
d) calling;
5. What tense is it?
“Modern pigs are found across Europe and extend into Asia?”
a) Past Indefinite Passive;
b) Present Indefinite Passive;
c) Present Perfect Passive;
d) Past Perfect Passive;
6. “The pot-bellied pig is a breed of domesticated pig originating ____ Vietnam with fourteen sub-species”.
a) in;
b) at;
c) from;
d) out of;
7. What word is wrong? “Some religious groups considers pork unclean”.
a) some;
b) pork;
c) considers;
d) unclean;
8. “Islam forbids the eating of flesh of swine or pork in any form ____ of its
uncleanliness”.
a) before;
b) because;
c) after;
d) when;
9. “Feral pigs have been introduced ____ many parts of the world”.
a) at;
b) with;
c) on;
d) into;
10. “George Clooney was famously known to have owned a potbellied pig named Max, although Max ____ in December 2006”.
a) dies;
b) died;
c) has died;
d) had died;
