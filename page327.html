Test I. Farm animal. The swine (I)

ТESTS OF LIVESTOCK PRODUCTION AND PROCESSING TECHNOLOGIES OF ANIMAL PRODUCTS

USE ALL YOUR KNOWLEDGES FOR DOING THIS TEST!

Choose the correct word in each of the following:
1. Synonym to “pig”.
a) hog;
b) chordate;
c) vertebrate;
d) animal.
2. “Pigs are watched by ____ ”.
a) swine
b) swineherds
c) livestock
d) their own young
3. “Pigs have a full set of ____ teeth”.
a) 22
b) 33
c) 44
d) 55
4. “Pigs are known for their ____ ”.
a) interest
b) intonation
c) interview
d) intelligence
5.“Pigs are ____, which means that they consume both plants and animals”.
a) ungulates
b) suidae
c) omnivores
d) peccaries
6. “In the wild, pigs are ____ animals”.
a) meat-eating
b) foraging
c) pot-bellied
d) parasite
7. “Pigs harbour a range of parasites and diseases that can be ____ to humans”.
a) used
b) allowed
c) introduced
d) transmitted
8. “Intensive piggeries are a type of ____ specialized for the raising of domestic pigs”.
a) factory farm
b) house
c) warehouse
d) storehouse
9. “Pigs are generally fed a combination of grain and ____ sources”.
a) fat
b) carbohydrate
c) protein
d) water
10. “A “sow stall” is the name of the confinement system for ____ sows”.
a) piglet
b) adult
c) shoat
d) boar
